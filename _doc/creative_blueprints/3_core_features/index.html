Core Features

Med Spa Owner Staff Member

Everything a med spa needs. Nothing it doesn't.

Dashboard

Real-time KPIs: leads, conversions, confirmation rate, no-show rate, and AI-influenced revenue with live activity feed.

Lead Management

Filter by status, date, treatment. Full detail page: message history, appointment timeline, editable notes, CSV import/export.

Appointments

Monthly/weekly/daily calendar. Confirm, cancel, reschedule, mark no-show. Send manual reminders from any appointment.

AI Automation

4 n8n workflows: lead follow-up (3–7 steps), appointment reminders (48h/24h/2h), re-engagement, review requests. Toggle + template each.

Messaging Center

Unified SMS + email inbox. Per-patient conversation threads, manual send, AI message history, reusable template library.

Reports & Analytics

Pre-built reports + custom builder. Line, bar, pie charts. Conversion by treatment, no-show trends, AI revenue impact. CSV/PDF export.

Billing & Settings

Plan management (Starter/Growth/Pro), org config, staff roles, Twilio + n8n integration settings, account security.

Auth & Access

Supabase Auth with email/password or OAuth. Role-based access (Owner / Staff) enforced via Row Level Security.
